FluxPulse: New inference from improved data processing

FluxPulse lets users upgrade the standard eddy-covariance processing with a particular focus on hard-to-capture responses to rain pulses, and examine how improved flux workflows change interpreted ecosystem signals. Explore spatial and temporal patterns directly in the app, then compare outcomes across processing choices for deeper interpretation.
